### Earned Income Deduction
Twenty percent of earned income is deducted.

### Maximum Allowable Resource Limits (Non-Categorically Eligible households)
1. $3,750 for FNS units with a member age 60 or older or disabled.
2. $2,500 for all other FNS units.
3. Households with $3,750 or more in gambling/lottery/bingo/cash prize winnings are no longer eligible.

### Excess Shelter Deduction
Maximum excess deduction for FNS units without a specified person: $597/month. No limit for units with a specified person.

### Homeless Shelter Allowance
Standard deduction: $159.73/month.

## 360.04 Calculation of Benefit Level

Uses the DSS-1700 Application Worksheet for manual calculation (system failure or trial budget scenarios).

**Conversion rules:**
- Weekly income × 4.3 = monthly
- Bi-weekly income × 2.15 = monthly
- Semi-monthly income × 2 = monthly

**Rounding:** Round to two decimals. Round down if third decimal ≤4, round up if ≥5.

**Minimum monthly allotment:** $20 for all one- and two-person FNS units, before proration.

**Proration formula (initial month):**
```
(31 - Date of Application) × Full Month's Benefit
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                     30
```
If prorated allotment is less than $10, issue no benefits for the initial month (but do not deny the application if it prorates to zero).
