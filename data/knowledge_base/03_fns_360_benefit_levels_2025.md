### Earned Income Deduction
Twenty percent of earned income is deducted.

### Maximum Allowable Resource Limits (Non-Categorically Eligible households)
1. $4,500 for FNS units with a member age 60 or older or disabled.
2. $3,000 for all other FNS units.
3. Households with $4,500 or more in gambling/lottery/bingo/cash prize winnings are no longer eligible.

### Excess Shelter Deduction
Maximum excess deduction for FNS units without a specified person: $744/month. No limit for units with a specified person (see Section 212.02, Specified Person).

### Homeless Shelter Allowance
Standard deduction: $198.99/month.

**Homeless Individual definition:** An individual who lacks a fixed and regular night-time residence, or whose primary night-time residence is:
1. A supervised shelter designed to provide temporary accommodation
2. A halfway house or similar institution for individuals intended to be institutionalized
3. Temporary accommodation in another individual's residence for no more than 90 days
4. A place not designed for regular sleeping accommodation (hallway, bus station, lobby, etc.)

To qualify for the homeless shelter allowance, the household must be responsible for or reasonably expect to incur shelter costs during the month.

## 360.04 Calculation of Benefit Level

Uses the DSS-1700 Application Worksheet for manual calculation (system failure or trial budget scenarios).

**Conversion rules:**
- Weekly income × 4.3 = monthly
- Bi-weekly income × 2.15 = monthly
- Semi-monthly income × 2 = monthly

**Rounding:** Round to two decimals. Round down if third decimal ≤4, round up if ≥5.

**Minimum monthly allotment:** $24 for all one- and two-person FNS units, before proration.

**Proration formula (initial month):**
```
(31 - Date of Application) × Full Month's Benefit
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                     30
```
If prorated allotment is less than $10, issue no benefits for the initial month (but do not deny the application if it prorates to zero).

**Worked example:** Application date is the 18th, monthly allotment is $256:
```
(31-18) × $256 / 30 = (13 × $256) / 30 = $3,328 / 30 = $110.00 prorated allotment
```
